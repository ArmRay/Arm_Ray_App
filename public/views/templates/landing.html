Be Productive On Day One

Create a project in seconds

With the click of a button, you can have your project up and running. After you’ve filled in a few simple variables it’s time to build your team.

Build your team in days, not months

ArmRay makes building a team simple. Search by a field of study, name, or any other criteria. Then add them to the project with the click of a button.

Take control of your team

Every project is private by default. This puts you in control of who does what on your team. You set the level of involvement for all of your collaborators.
